His Highness, the prince of Al-Madinah Al-Munawwarah Region, patronizes the
inauguration ceremony of the Islamic University of its third strategic plan 2021-2025


His Royal Highness Prince Faisal bin Salman bin Abdulaziz, Governor of Madinah Region, sponsored the inauguration ceremony of the
third strategic plan for the Islamic University 2021-2025, which the university worked on preparing using the Balanced Scorecard
(BSC) methodology according to its four perspectives and appropriate to the nature of the university, which bears the slogan
" Your role is important."


His Highness, the President of the University, Dr. Mamdouh bin Saud bin Thunayan Al Saud, explained, during the speech ceremony
held for this occasion in the University’s Peace Hall, in the presence of the university’s vice deans, deans of faculties and support
deans, general managers and a number of officials, that this plan was prepared by a team of university employees. And an advisory
team from outside the university, according to the methodological foundations of strategic planning, as the Balanced Scorecard
methodology and its four dimensions were adopted in line with the nature of the university and its educational, research and societal
role. Achieving integration and partnerships, and for the university graduate to be a global graduate with a high level of knowledge
and skills.
