About Deanship

Foundation

The Higher Education Council approved the establishment of the Deanship of Alumni Affairs by Resolution No. (32/49/1429 AH) at its forty-ninth session held on 4/2/1429 AH. In 1441 AH, a decision was issued by His Excellency the Minister of Education to change the name of the Deanship to the Deanship of Graduates and International Cooperation.

Vision

For the Deanship of Alumni and International Cooperation to be a global model for communication with graduates and cooperation with local and international institutions.

Mision

Strengthening the university’s relationship with its graduates, and supporting aspects of cooperation with institutions locally and internationally; Using the latest technology and international quality standards

Objectives

Enhancing the depth of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and its position among graduates


Strengthening the university’s relationship with its graduates and developing their scientific and practical capabilities.


Encouraging and coordinating cooperation programs in various academic and service fields between the university and local and international institutions.


Achieving excellence and quality in the areas of communication with graduates and cooperation with institutions locally and internationally
